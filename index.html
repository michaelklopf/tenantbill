Introduction

With this application you can set a timeframe in between a year to compute the remaining costs a tenant has to pay after the heating bill has been evaluated.

It allows to set a date in between a year, to accomodate the fact a tenant has moved in the property during the year.

Enter the total costs, the amount of pre-payments the tenant did, and if necessary add the costs you cannot hand down to the tenant.
